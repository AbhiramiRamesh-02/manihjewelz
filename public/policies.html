Policies & Compliance

Terms, policies, and details regarding your purchases from Manih Jewelz

Shipping Policy

Thank you for shopping with us! We are committed to ensuring your order reaches you safely and as quickly as possible.

Order Processing

  • All orders are processed within 1–3 business days after successful payment confirmation.
  • Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.
  • Once your order is packed and handed over to our courier partner, you will receive a shipping confirmation along with your tracking details.

Shipping Charges

  • A flat shipping fee of ₹50 is applicable on all orders across India.
  • The shipping charge will be displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

Estimated Delivery Time

Estimated delivery times are as follows:

  • Metro Cities: 2–5 business days
  • Other Cities & Towns: 4–8 business days
  • Remote Locations: 5–10 business days

Please note that these are estimated delivery timelines and may vary due to courier delays, weather conditions, festivals, or other unforeseen circumstances.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking link or tracking number via email or WhatsApp (if provided), allowing you to monitor your shipment's progress.

Delivery Address

Please ensure that your shipping address, contact number, and other details are accurate when placing your order. We are not responsible for delays or failed deliveries caused by incorrect or incomplete information provided by the customer.

Failed Delivery Attempts

If the courier is unable to deliver your order due to an incorrect address, customer unavailability, or refusal to accept the package, the order may be returned to us. Re-shipping charges may apply if the order needs to be sent again.

Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or appears to be lost in transit, please contact us within 48 hours of the delivery date. Kindly include your order number and clear photos of the package (if damaged). We will work with our courier partner to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Delays Beyond Our Control

While we strive to deliver all orders on time, delays caused by courier partners, natural disasters, strikes, public holidays, weather conditions, or other unforeseen events are beyond our control. We appreciate your patience and understanding in such situations.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your order or shipping, please feel free to contact our customer support team through our website or email. We will be happy to assist you.

Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping with us. We take great care in packaging every order to ensure it reaches you in perfect condition.

Returns

We accept returns only if the product is received damaged.

Returns will not be accepted for:

  • Change of mind
  • Incorrect product selection by the customer
  • Personal preferences (such as size, color, or design expectations)
  • Normal wear and tear after use

Reporting a Damaged Product

If you receive a damaged product, you must contact us within 48 hours of delivery.

To be eligible for a return and refund, you must provide:

  • Your order number.
  • A clear unboxing video showing the sealed package being opened from the beginning.
  • Clear photos of the damaged product.

Important: The unboxing video must be continuous, unedited, and recorded from the moment the sealed package is opened. Claims made without a valid unboxing video may not be eligible for return or refund.

Return Approval

Once we review the evidence provided, we will notify you whether your return request has been approved.

If approved:

  • We will arrange a return pickup (where service is available) or provide return instructions.
  • The product must be unused and returned with its original packaging and all accessories (if any).

Refunds

Once the returned product is received and inspected, your refund will be processed.

Approved refunds will be credited to your original payment method within 5–7 business days after the refund is initiated. The exact time for the amount to reflect in your account may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be provided if:

  • The product is not damaged.
  • The damage occurred after delivery due to misuse or improper handling.
  • The request is made after 48 hours of delivery.
  • A valid unboxing video and supporting photos are not provided.
  • The returned product is found to have been used, altered, or damaged after delivery.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding returns or refunds, please contact our customer support team through our website or email. We are happy to assist you.
